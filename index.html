UW Product Vision

The goal of Product Vision club is to foster product leadership amongst tech students.

What we do:

  • Educational Sessions

    These sessions will involve: Learning about product management (PM) internship experiences,Learning about how to get a PM placement, and Learning about how to prepare for PM internship/full­time interviews

  • Guest Speakers

    Hearing about how it’s like to work as a PM in the industry: Past speakers have came from various organizations including Quora,Square, FireFox, Clearpath Robotics, Magnetic Forensics, Microsoft,Groupon, etc. Opportunies to hear about how the speakers became PM’s and general informal conversation and Question sessions

  • Waterloo Trek

    Trek Program: Exclusive trek program that brings a select group of UWaterloo students to visit and interact with leadership from world leading technology companies, Exclusive networking opportunity with industry leaders, Opportunity to build the next generation trek program for UWaterloo
