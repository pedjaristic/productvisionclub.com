What we do:

  • Educational Sessions

    - Past sessions led by interns and full-time incoming PMs from Microsoft, Groupon etc.
    - Hear about product management (PM) internship experiences and lessons
    - Learn how to prepare for interviews and land PM coop and full-time placements

  • Guest Speakers

    -Past speakers have come from top companies including Quora, Square, Mozilla, Clearpath Robotics, Magnet Forensics, Microsoft, Groupon etc.
    - Hear about how it’s like to work as a PM/engineer in the industry
    - Learn about speaker’s experiences and their journey to become PM’s
    - General career advice, product perspectives and interactive Q&A sessions

  • Innovation Trek

    - Past treks have included meeting with the Creator of BBM, Co-founders of Vidyard and a Google PM
    - Application based program for selected Trek Fellows to interact with leaders from top technology companies
    - Exclusive networking opportunity to get first-hand advice and learn from some of the best people from industry
    - Connect with other like-minded top students with diverse talents and join our fellows and alumni network for career opportunities and advice

Upcoming Event: (Spring 2015)

Product Management Case Competition

- Get a chance to apply your skills to research and propose products/features
- Gain valuable experience to prepare for interviews and future PM placements
- Develop an entrepreneurial mindset and foster product perspective to build great products that people want
